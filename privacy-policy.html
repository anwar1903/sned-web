Privacy Policy

Sned is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information.

1. No Personal Data Collected

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from users of the Sned app. All file transfers occur directly between your devices over a local network.

2. No Cloud Storage

Sned does not use any cloud services or remote servers. Your files never leave your devices during transfers.

3. No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics tools, or any form of tracking in the Sned app.

4. Third-Party Services

Sned does not integrate with third-party services that collect user data.

5. Children's Privacy

Sned is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at peopleappz@gmail.com.
